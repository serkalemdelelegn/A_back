About Us

Arba Minch City Chamber of Commerce and Sectoral Associations!

The Arba Minch City Chamber of Commerce and Sectoral Associations play a pivotal role in driving economic growth, promoting entrepreneurship, and facilitating collaboration within the business community. In this two-page description, we explore the functions, initiatives, and impact of these organizations in Arba Minch.

Our Services

Business development programs

Providing training, mentorship, and support services to aspiring entrepreneurs and small businesses to enhance their competitiveness and sustainability.

Advocacy campaigns>

Engaging with government authorities, policymakers, and other stakeholders to address regulatory barriers, promote investment incentives, and advocate for pro-business policies.

Market access initiatives>

Facilitating trade missions, business matchmaking events, and market linkage programs to connect local businesses with domestic and international markets, thereby expanding their reach and opportunities for growth.

Sector-specific interventions>

Working closely with industry stakeholders to identify sector-specific challenges and opportunities, develop tailored solutions, and promote the growth and competitiveness of key sectors such as agriculture, tourism, manufacturing, and services.

Membership

The Arba Minch City Chamber of Commerce and Sectoral Associations fosters economic growth by providing resources, advocacy, and networking opportunities to its members.

Fees

The membership fees for the Arba Minch City Chamber of Commerce are structured to be affordable and are used to support the various programs and services offered to the business community.


Benefits

Members of the chamber gain access to a wide range of benefits, including networking opportunities, business development programs, advocacy support, and market access initiatives.


How to Apply

To apply for membership, businesses can fill out the application form available on the chamber's website or visit the chamber's office for assistance. Required documents include business registration and proof of address.

Arbitration

The Legal & Arbitration Service is a department tasked with, as the name itself indicates, affairs relating legal and arbitration. Major services offered (functions) by the Department/Division are:-

Legal Service: Advising the Board of Directors, the Secretariat, departments and divisions; Representing ECCSA at courts of different levels; drafting legal documents; extending advice to members of the business community and give the required support to, specially, those embarking on import/export activities; are chief to mention.

Arbitration Service: Facilitating forum for arbitration, conducting annual workshop on Alternative Dispute Resolution, Renting out Arbitration room for ad hoc arbitration; are some of the services well undertaken by the department.

Trade Facilitation

As part of ECCSA’s Core functions, The Trade Facilitation Division supports the private sector, particularly Exporters relating to export trade. The Division works with exporters, whether first-time or experienced, to identify their opportunities and to solve their challenges. Moreover, the Trade Facilitation Division supports the simplification and associated information flows required to move goods from seller to buyer. The Division desires to improve the trade environment and reduce the transaction cost of exporters. In order to achieve these activities, the Chamber works closely with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Customs Commission, and other relevant organizations.

Major services offered (description)

  • Certificate of Origin and COMESA certificate of Origin
  • Commercial Invoice
  • Packing list
  • Document authentication
  • Recommendation letter to Embassy
